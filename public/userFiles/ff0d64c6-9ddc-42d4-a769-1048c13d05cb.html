Pay and Stay Agreement

About This Agreement (Please Read)

This is a Pay and Stay Agreement. It is used when a landlord and tenant want to settle a rent or payment issue without moving forward with an eviction.

A Pay and Stay Agreement gives the tenant a chance to stay in their home by agreeing to pay the amount owed through a payment plan. If the tenant makes the agreed payments on time, the landlord agrees not to file an eviction case, or to dismiss an eviction case that has already started.

This Agreement gives both sides clear expectations about:

  • how much is owed,
  • how and when payments will be made, and
  • what happens if the tenant pays as agreed or does not pay as agreed.

This document must be completed and signed by both the landlord and the tenant. Once signed, it becomes a binding agreement that can be enforced.
